# The 17 Sheathed Sword Sentences

These sentences are not persuasion weapons. They are agency-strengthening language patterns.

| # | Sentence | Pattern |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | You do not have to disappear to keep peace. | Dignity restoration |
| 2 | Your no is allowed, and your yes should be free. | Consent strengthening |
| 3 | I believe you can choose clearly when you have room to breathe. | Courage activation |
| 4 | What you feel makes sense from where you are standing. | Experience validation |
| 5 | We can slow this down and still care about each other. | Relational safety |
| 6 | I can disagree with you without withdrawing respect. | Truth with mercy |
| 7 | Let us name the fear without letting it drive. | Fear separation |
| 8 | What would feel honest, kind, and sustainable? | Wise choice invitation |
| 9 | You are allowed to need clarity before commitment. | Discernment blessing |
| 10 | A boundary is not a rejection of love. | Boundary blessing |
| 11 | Let us separate what happened from what we fear it means. | Fog cutting |
| 12 | What part of you is trying to protect something important? | Compassionate inquiry |
| 13 | You are not wrong for needing time. | Pace restoration |
| 14 | We can tell the truth without making it cruel. | Truth with mercy |
| 15 | What would become possible if shame was not in charge? | Shame dissolving |
| 16 | I want your real answer, not your pressured answer. | Consent strengthening |
| 17 | Let us choose the path that leaves dignity intact. | Dignity restoration |

# Safety and Claim Boundaries

LumenSword369 is an education and reflection project.

## It does not claim to

- guarantee emotional outcomes
- replace therapy, legal advice, medical advice, or crisis support
- make people agree
- diagnose conversations or relationships
- prove what someone should do

## It does claim to

- provide empowering sentence patterns
- support consent-aware communication
- encourage truth with mercy
- strengthen dignity, agency, and possibility
- offer reflection prompts for clearer conversation

## Safety reminder

If there is immediate danger, threats, stalking, coercive control, self-harm risk, or fear for physical safety, do not rely on a script. Prioritize trusted support, local emergency services, or qualified professional help.
